    Our Cameras

    CAMERA 1

    Visual Sensors

    • High resolution still & Video camera with Low Distortion
    • A visible light camera used for capturing RGB color image
    • Used for orthophotography by combining series of images to generate a 3D model

    Technical Specifications

    • Effective Pixels 18.4 Megapixels
    • Ground Resolution 1.3 cm/pixel (at 50m)
    • Focal length10 mm
    • Pixels 2.864 microns

    Uses

    • Aerial mapping and imaging
    • Photogrammetry and 3D reconstruction
    • Plant counting
    • Surveillance
    • Emergency response
    • Surveying and land use applications

    About the Camera

    Camera captures a photo every 3 seconds. For best results, we survey overlap of at least 85% front-lap and 70% side-lap and a maximum 5f/s (1.5m/s) speed for every 65ft (20m) of altitude (AGL)
